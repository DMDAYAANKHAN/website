The Seven Continents Of The World

Europe

  • Europe is a landmass located entirely in the Northern Hemisphere and mostly in the Eastern Hemisphere.
  • It comprises the westernmost peninsulas of the continental landmass of Eurasia.
  • it shares the continental landmass of Afro-Eurasia with both Asia and Africa and is bordered by the Arctic Ocean to the north.
  • Europe covers about 10,180,000 km2 (3,930,000 sq mi), or 2% of the Earth's surface.
  • Politically, Europe is divided into about fifty sovereign states, of which Russia is the largest and most populous.
