The Seven Continents Of The World

Oceania

  • Oceania is a geographic region
  • Oceania includes Australasia, Melanesia, Micronesia and Polynesia.
  • Oceania was first explored by Europeans from the 16th century onward.
  • The first settlers of Australia, New Guinea, and the large islands just to the east arrived more than 60,000 years ago.
  • Spanning the Eastern and Western Hemispheres, Oceania has a land area of 8,525,989 square kilometres (3,291,903 sq mi) and a population of over 41 million.
