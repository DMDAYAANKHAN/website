The Seven Continents Of The World

North America

  • North America is a continent in the Northern Hemisphere and almost entirely within the Western Hemisphere.
  • It can also be described as the northern subcontinent of a single continent, America.
  • North America covers an area of about 24,709,000 square kilometers (9,540,000 square miles), about 16.5% of the Earth's land area.
  • North America is the third-largest continent by area.
  • North America was reached by its first human populations during the last glacial period, via crossing the Bering land bridge approximately 40,000 to 17,000 years ago.
